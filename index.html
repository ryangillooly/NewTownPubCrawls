About Us

  • Rhys Jones
  • Jack Ashton

We are a team of 3 from Edinburgh who love to bring people together, make new friends and of course, get drunk!
We are university students trying to branch out and create a unique, organised pub crawl experience! Especially for folks that are new to Edinburgh or just visiting!

We run the pub crawls every Wednesday, Friday & Saturday from 8pm until 3am ... if you can keep going until then!
